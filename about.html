About us

Founded and nestled in the heart of the Shenandoah Valley, Greens and Grains believes in a "eat fresh, support local" philosophy. We have a wide selection of menu items that appeal to any palate with a focus on cleaner eating. Our ingredients come from locally sourced farms and vendors; we pride ourselves on using the earth's ingredients to make our restaurant unique and great!

Our Values

Hospitality

Greens and Grains cafe recognizes that each customer has unique dietery needs. Many of our recipes are gluten free and can accomodate vegan and vegetarian needs. Please ask if you have such requirements!

Locally Sourced Ingredients

Since we opened our doors, we have taken pride in providing the freshest ingredients from local farms, abiding by our "fresh is fabulous" philosophy. Come check us out, we think you will agree.

Sustainability

In addition to advocating a healthier lifestyle with the fresh menu items we offer, Greens and Grains Cafe believes in environmental stewardship, with an emphasis on reusable utensils and biodegradable straws.
